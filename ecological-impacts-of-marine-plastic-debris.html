Ecological impacts of marine plastic debris

by Hannah Ritchie and Max Roser

The table below provides a summary of peer-reviewed evidence of marine plastic impact on animal life based on analysis by Rocham et al. (2016).1 The review is based on publications through the year 2013. This data is also presented in the review by Law (2017).2

MF is an abbreviation for monofilament line.
